Eadartrans Wins "Outstanding Translation Service Enterprise" Award

On May 20, 2026, in the annual translation industry selection event, Eadartrans (Wuhan) Co., Ltd. won the "Outstanding Translation Service Enterprise" award for its excellent service quality, professional translation team, and good customer reputation. This honor is a high recognition of the company's commitment to high-quality development over the years.

Strict Selection Criteria

This selection event was hosted by the industry association, with evaluation criteria covering service quality, customer satisfaction, professional team building, and technological innovation capabilities. The selection process included enterprise application, document review, customer visits, and expert review to ensure the fairness and authority of the results.

Leading Customer Satisfaction

In the customer satisfaction survey, Eadartrans achieved a 99% customer satisfaction rating. Customers generally recognize the company's professional level, response speed, and service attitude. Many long-term cooperation clients stated that Eadartrans's translation quality is stable and reliable, making it a trustworthy language service partner.

Professional Team Strength

The company has a team of more than 50 professional translators, all graduates from well-known domestic and international universities with rich industry experience and professional backgrounds. The team covers more than 50 languages including English, Japanese, Korean, German, French, and Russian, meeting clients' diverse language service needs.

Future Outlook

Winning the "Outstanding Translation Service Enterprise" award is an affirmation of the company's past achievements and an inspiration for future development. Eadartrans will continue to uphold the service philosophy of "Professional, Precise, and Efficient," continuously improve service quality, create greater value for clients, and contribute to industry development.

Eadartrans (Wuhan) Co., Ltd. is a professional language service provider dedicated to delivering high-quality, accurate, and efficient language solutions to clients worldwide.
