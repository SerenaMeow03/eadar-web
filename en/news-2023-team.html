Company News September 10, 2023

Eadartrans Announces Major Team Expansion and New Office Opening

Eadartrans (Wuhan) Co., Ltd. is pleased to announce a significant milestone in our company's growth journey. As of September 2023, we have successfully expanded our professional translation team and opened a new state-of-the-art office facility in Wuhan's Donghu New Technology Development Zone to better serve our growing client base.

Team Expansion Highlights

In response to increasing demand for high-quality language services, we have expanded our team by adding 15 new professional translators and project managers. This expansion brings our total team strength to over 50 dedicated professionals, enabling us to handle larger volumes of work while maintaining our commitment to quality and timely delivery.

New Team Members Include:

  • Senior Medical Translators: Three specialists with extensive experience in pharmaceutical and clinical trial documentation
  • Legal Translation Experts: Two professionals with backgrounds in international law and contract translation
  • Technical Writers: Four specialists in engineering, IT, and manufacturing documentation
  • Project Managers: Three experienced coordinators to ensure seamless workflow management
  • Quality Assurance Specialists: Two dedicated professionals for enhanced quality control
  • Business Development: One new team member to strengthen client relationships

New Office Facility

Our new office, located in the heart of Wuhan's technology corridor, represents a significant upgrade in our working environment. The 800-square-meter facility features:

  • Modern, ergonomic workstations designed for translator productivity
  • Secure document handling areas with controlled access
  • Advanced IT infrastructure with redundant systems for data security
  • Collaborative meeting spaces for team discussions and client consultations
  • Quiet zones for focused translation work
  • Client reception area for in-person meetings

Commitment to Quality

This expansion is not merely about increasing capacity—it's about enhancing our capabilities to deliver even better service to our clients. Each new team member has undergone rigorous screening and testing to ensure they meet Eadartrans's high standards for professional competence and ethical conduct.

"Our growth is a direct result of the trust our clients place in us," said the company founder. "This expansion allows us to maintain our personalized service approach while handling more complex, large-scale projects. We're excited about the opportunities this brings for both our team and our clients."

Looking Forward

With our expanded team and enhanced facilities, Eadartrans is well-positioned to continue our trajectory of growth and excellence. We remain committed to our core values of accuracy, reliability, and client satisfaction as we enter this new chapter of our company's development.

We invite our clients and partners to visit our new office and meet our expanded team. Thank you for your continued trust and support in Eadartrans.

Eadartrans (Wuhan) Co., Ltd. is a professional language service provider dedicated to delivering high-quality, accurate, and efficient language solutions to clients worldwide.
