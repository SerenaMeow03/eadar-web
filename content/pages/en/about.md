# About Eadar Translation

## Our History

Founded in 2014 as a professional translation studio, Eadar Translation (Wuhan) Co., Ltd. was officially registered in 2020 after 6 years of team building and development.

## Our Mission

To bridge language barriers and help businesses succeed in the global market through professional, accurate, and efficient translation services.

## Our Team

- **Professional Translators**: 30+ covering major languages
- **Review Experts**: 10+ industry specialists
- **Project Managers**: 5+ ensuring smooth delivery
- **Technical Support**: 5+ providing technical assistance

## Our Values

- **Professionalism**: Expert translators in every field
- **Efficiency**: Fast turnaround without compromising quality
- **Integrity**: Transparent pricing and honest communication

## Certifications

- ISO 17100 Translation Services Certification
- Professional translation quality management system

We are committed to providing the highest quality language services to our clients worldwide.
