About Us

Eadartrans is a professional language service provider dedicated to helping clients break language barriers, convey messages accurately, and empower global business development.

10+ Years of Experience

Professional Language Services
Connecting Global Markets

Eadartrans (Wuhan) Co., Ltd. was officially registered and established in 2020, with headquarters at Building 9, Guanggu Entrepreneurship Street, Donghu New Technology Development Zone, Wuhan, China. Our team began building in 2014, co-founded by alumni from Shanghai International Studies University, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Ohio State University, and University of Cambridge.

We are dedicated to providing competitive translation services to domestic and international enterprises, government agencies, and academic researchers, enhancing our clients' image and creating greater value for them. Our clients span numerous industries including biomedicine, chemical, medical, electromechanical, automotive, aviation, marine, fashion, construction, legal, IT, and finance.

After years of development, we have grown into a professional language service provider covering 50+ languages, with a cumulative translation volume exceeding 500 million words and a customer satisfaction rate of 99%.
